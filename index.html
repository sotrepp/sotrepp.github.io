The Past

2 million years ago, the earth was unspoiled

Trees grew, animals roamed freely. There was no pollution, global warming or deforestation. The sky was clean and so were the oceans.
It's a place that once existed, it's a place we should be inspired by.

The Present
Technology defines our world today

Technology may contribute to a comfortable life, however what we see is just the tip of the iceberg: Irresponsible use of modern technology contribute to global warming, pushing humanity to the brink of environmental destruction.
This needs to change: together we must step up and note that even the smallest achievement counts towards progress.
